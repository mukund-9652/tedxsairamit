TED

TED is a non-profit forum which aims to build up a sociable, robust and a sustainable community. They catalyse thinking under the banner “IDEAS WORTH SPREADING”. TED signifies technology, entertainment and design. Speakers from various branches of knowledge share their experience and wisdom. Their words do give life to diverse designs. The speakers get through the spectators and encourage their thoughts ambiguously. It’s purely a gathering that brings fascinating, distinct individuals together. This collaboration germinates new calibre. TED is a major supporting tool as it adapts to gradual evolution of learning.

TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized. (Subject to certain rules and regulations.) For more information on TEDx, visit http://www.ted.com/tedx.

Sri Sai Ram Institute of Technology

Sri Sai Ram Institute of Technology moves ahead towards the path of excellence in diversified domains with inspiring ambience for rational developments, novel thinking, and personal growth to prepare the scholars with the skills, insights and practical experience to serve the society. Besides, it provides leading–edge opportunities to excel in all aspects like Academic,Professional, Leadership development to face the Global challenges. The Institution encourages an excellent all-around education and exposure. Sri Sai Ram Institute of Technology is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). It is recertified as ISO 9001:2015 Certified institution as well, it has been accredited with “A+” grade with a CGPA of 3.30 by the Executive Committee of NAAC.
